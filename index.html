Our Story

Premium Burgers,
Made for Houston

Tucked into the heart of Rice Village, Hopdoddy Burger Bar serves fresh, made-to-order burgers built on locally sourced ingredients and a relentless commitment to quality.

From Wagyu beef and creative chef-driven specials to a secret menu that rewards the curious, every plate is hand-crafted with care. Pair it with hand-cut Parmesan Truffle Fries and a real-deal milkshake, and you've got the kind of meal worth the trip.

Locally Sourced

Fresh produce and proteins from trusted Texas suppliers.

Hand-Smashed

Every patty is cooked to order on a smoking-hot griddle.
